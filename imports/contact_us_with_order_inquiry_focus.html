Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about your orders.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also enter your Order ID and Email on our tracking page to see live updates.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact support with your Order ID to initiate a return.

Do I need an account to buy?

No, you can check out as a guest. All order details and updates will be sent directly to the email address you provide during checkout.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within the US typically takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for 1-2 day delivery.
